Daily Self-Care Tips

Morning Ritual: Start your day with 5 minutes of stretching and deep breathing to set a positive tone.
Evening Wind-down: Create a calming bedtime routine, like reading or gentle yoga, to improve sleep quality.
Mindful Eating: Pay attention to your meals, eating slowly and savoring each bite for better digestion and satisfaction.
Social Connection: Reach out to a friend or family member daily, even if it's just a quick call or message.
Nature Time: Spend at least 10 minutes outdoors each day to reduce stress and boost mood.
